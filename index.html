You play a doctor who has to share his knowledge to people in risk of infection, the people speak to each other to transmit the knowledge.
Some npc start sick and spread the disease by speaking to others with less knowledge level that the infector's disease level.
Those whith a higher level of knowledge than their disease level will no longer spread it.
